Founder & Director

Mr. Zahid Khan

Founder, Z.K Next Gen School

Mr. Zahid Khan founded Z.K Next Gen School with a clear goal: to provide quality education that combines modern learning with strong values. With over 15 years of experience in education and community development, he has led the school to become a trusted name for parents and students. His focus is on creating a safe, inclusive environment where every child can excel academically and grow as a responsible citizen.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops confident, capable, and morally grounded students. We aim to prepare the next generation with strong academic skills, digital literacy, and Islamic values so they can succeed in life and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading school where every child is valued and supported. We envision a learning community that blends modern education with tradition, producing well-rounded individuals who are ready for higher education, work, and responsible citizenship.

Who We Are

Z.K Next Gen School offers a balanced curriculum that prepares students for today’s world while keeping our values at the centre.

  • Quality academic programs aligned with national standards.
  • Computer and digital skills training for all age groups.
  • Islamic studies, Quran education, and moral training.
  • Personality development, workshops, and community programs.
